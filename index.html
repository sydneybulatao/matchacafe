OUR STORY...

We're a small Boston-based cafe specializing in matcha. Our idea was to create a fun and modern cafe that focused on creating the best quality matcha lattes imaginable, and we believe we've done just that!
Our menu is set up so that you can fully customize your matcha - from choosing the type of milk to adding a shot of espresso!

Whether you sit and stay a while or just grab and go, we hope that your day will be brightened by visiting us!



ABOUT OUR MATCHA...

We use ceremonial grade matcha grown in Japan.
100% pure green tea leaves ground into a fine powder. No sweeteners or sugar added!
